introduction

IOM is an intergovernmental organization established in 1951, IOM is committed to the principle that humane and orderly migration benefits migrants and society.

Migration Health Department (MHD) Islamabad was established in 2001 under IOM Pakistan in continuation of modest health activities, expanded in October 2005 due to Emergency Health Program and has further evolved since 2007 after getting the mandate to perform the TB Screening for UK visa applicants.
